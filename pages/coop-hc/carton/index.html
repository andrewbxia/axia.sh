guidelines

to qualify a journal as meaningful work, it should be:

  • express something about you!
  • either be different from the rest of the site, or a significant update to an existing feature!
  • If you're not following tutorials, have thought/creativity behind it!
    • if you followed a tutorial, you still need to add a feature to your homepage; we just won't hold it to the same standards. Make sure to mention it in your journal!
